Capability Statement

Your Partner for Residential & Commercial Construction and Expert Consultancy Across New South Wales

About CALDON™

CALDON™ is a leading building consultancy and construction firm in New South Wales, offering both bespoke residential and commercial building solutions alongside strategic consultancy services. Headquartered in Taree, we proudly serve clients in Port Macquarie, Coffs Harbour, Newcastle, Sydney, Tamworth, Forster Tuncurry, and beyond.

With Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873, Builders Licence 377088C, and membership in the Housing Industry Association (HIA), we uphold the highest standards of professional excellence, regulatory compliance, and innovation. Our aim is to deliver outcomes that consistently exceed expectations—whether you’re seeking a dream home build or comprehensive commercial project guidance.

Capabilities and Services

CALDON™ provides a wide-ranging suite of services covering every aspect of construction and consultancy. From designing and building custom homes to overseeing large-scale commercial developments, we prioritize quality, efficiency, and collaboration.

Residential Design & Construction

We craft custom homes that reflect each client’s vision, integrating sustainable materials and thoughtful design for long-term value.

  • Architectural design and 3D modeling
  • Single and multi-storey bespoke builds
  • Eco-friendly material integration
  • Compliance with NSW Building Codes
  • Renovations and extensions

Class 2 & Multi-Residential

Our Class 2 expertise spans multi-unit developments of various scales, ensuring robust fire safety, accessibility, and structural compliance.

  • Design and construction of multi-unit complexes
  • Fire safety and accessibility compliance
  • Coordination with architects, engineers, and strata
  • Mid-rise and high-rise construction
  • Post-completion maintenance planning

Commercial Construction

We bring commercial spaces to life—offices, retail, and industrial builds—providing turnkey solutions that meet strict timelines and budgets.

  • Offices, retail, and industrial facilities
  • Fit-outs and refurbishments
  • Subcontractor coordination
  • Workplace health and safety adherence
  • On-time, on-budget delivery

Estimating & Cost Consulting

Our cost consulting services keep your project financially on track through accurate estimates and ongoing value engineering.

  • Feasibility analyses and cost breakdowns
  • Material take-offs and labor estimates
  • Risk assessment and contingency planning
  • Real-time financial tracking
  • Value management for optimal ROI

Expert Witness & Dispute Resolution

We offer legal expertise in construction disputes, providing forensic analysis, testimony, and mediation support across New South Wales.

  • Forensic investigation of defects and delays
  • Expert reports for litigation
  • Mediation and arbitration support
  • Contract compliance analysis
  • Trusted by legal firms and developers

Commercial Procurement

Simplify your supply chain with our procurement services, ensuring timely and cost-effective sourcing of quality materials.

  • Competitive tendering and supplier selection
  • Specialized equipment procurement
  • Cost optimization and contract management
  • Performance tracking
  • Sustainable sourcing options

Building Surveying & Compliance

Our thorough inspections and compliance checks protect your property’s integrity, ensuring adherence to all relevant standards.

  • Pre-purchase and defect inspections
  • Building code and Australian Standards audits
  • Dilapidation and condition reports
  • Occupancy and construction certification
  • Heritage building assessments

Quantity Surveying & Cost Management

Our quantity surveyors provide precise oversight, ensuring project budgets are met without compromising on quality.

  • Bills of quantities and cost breakdowns

  • Change order and budget tracking
  • Cash flow forecasting
  • Post-completion audits

Project Management

From scheduling to stakeholder communication, CALDON™ ensures seamless project delivery with a keen eye on quality and risk management.
