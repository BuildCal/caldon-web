Coastal Modern Residence in Taree

Project Overview

This contemporary coastal residence was designed to blend seamlessly with its natural surroundings while providing a luxurious yet comfortable living environment for a family of four.

Featuring open-plan living spaces that flow onto expansive decking, the home maximizes views of the surrounding landscape while maintaining privacy from neighboring properties.

Design Approach

The clients approached us with a vision for a contemporary home that would take advantage of the coastal views while providing comfortable living spaces for their growing family.

Our design team worked closely with the clients to develop a concept that balanced bold architectural elements with practical family living requirements. The result is a home that feels both impressive and welcoming.

Materials were selected for both their aesthetic appeal and durability in the coastal environment, with extensive use of natural timber, stone, and large-format glazing to create a seamless indoor-outdoor connection.

Early conceptual rendering showing the interplay of volumes and materials

Construction Challenges

The sloping site presented several challenges during the construction phase, requiring innovative engineering solutions to create level living spaces while minimizing excavation.

Our construction team implemented a stepped foundation system that followed the natural contours of the land, reducing the need for extensive retaining walls while ensuring structural integrity.

Harsh coastal conditions necessitated careful material selection and application techniques, particularly for exterior finishes and fixtures exposed to salt-laden air and strong winds.

Outdoor Living

Recognizing the importance of outdoor living in the Australian lifestyle, we designed extensive covered deck areas that function as extensions of the interior living spaces.

Weather-resistant materials and thoughtful orientation ensure these spaces remain comfortable and usable throughout the year, regardless of sun position or prevailing winds.

Strategic landscaping with native species complements the architecture while requiring minimal maintenance and water usage.

Covered deck areas provide year-round outdoor living space

Sustainable Features

Sustainability was integrated throughout the design and construction process, with careful consideration given to energy efficiency, water conservation, and material selection.

The home features passive solar design principles, high-performance insulation, and energy-efficient systems that significantly reduce operational costs and environmental impact.

Rainwater harvesting, solar hot water, and a 10kW photovoltaic system further reduce the home's ecological footprint while providing long-term cost benefits for the owners.

10kW solar array integrated with roof design for maximum efficiency

The Result

The completed residence stands as a testament to thoughtful design, quality construction, and collaborative client engagement. It successfully balances aesthetic aspirations with practical considerations, creating a home that will serve its owners beautifully for generations to come.

This project exemplifies our commitment to creating homes that respond sensitively to their context, meet the unique needs of their owners, and stand the test of time in both durability and design.
