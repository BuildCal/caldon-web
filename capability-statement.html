About Us

At Caldon Homes, we combine artistry and precision to create custom homes that fulfill your vision while meeting the highest standards of durability and design. For commercial clients, we offer unrivaled consultancy services that mitigate risks and optimize outcomes, drawing on our deep industry experience.

As registered practitioners (Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873) and proud members of the Housing Industry Association (HIA), we ensure every project reflects our commitment to excellence and professionalism.

Our Services

  • Residential Design & Construction: Custom home design and construction, from concept to completion, with a focus on bespoke aesthetics, structural integrity, and premium craftsmanship tailored to your unique needs.
  • Class 2 Building Construction: Specialized construction of multi-residential buildings, including apartments and townhouses, adhering to stringent codes and delivering durable, high-quality outcomes.
  • Estimating Services: Detailed cost estimation and budgeting for residential and commercial projects, ensuring transparency, accuracy, and financial control.
  • Expert Witness: Authoritative litigation support and expert testimony for construction disputes, leveraging extensive industry knowledge.
  • Commercial Procurement: Strategic procurement and tender management for commercial projects, securing optimal materials and subcontractor services.
  • Building Surveying: Thorough building inspections and compliance assessments, ensuring safety and adherence to Australian standards.
  • Quantity Surveying: Comprehensive cost management and financial planning, including bills of quantities and value engineering.
  • Project Management: End-to-end project oversight, coordinating timelines, budgets, and teams to deliver precision and quality.
