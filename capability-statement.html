Capability Statement

Delivering Excellence in Residential Construction and Commercial Consultancy Across NSW

About CALDON™

CALDON™ is a leading New South Wales firm specialising in high-quality residential construction and advanced commercial building consultancy services. Based in Taree, our expertise extends across key regions including Port Macquarie, Coffs Harbour, Newcastle, Sydney, Tamworth, and Forster Tuncurry, as well as surrounding areas. We are committed to delivering precision-crafted homes and strategic commercial solutions tailored to client needs.

With Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873, Builders Licence 377088C, and membership in the Housing Industry Association (HIA), CALDON™ upholds the highest standards of professionalism, compliance, and innovation in every project.

Capabilities and Services

CALDON™ provides a comprehensive range of services to residential and commercial clients throughout New South Wales. Our capabilities span bespoke home construction and sophisticated consultancy for complex developments, ensuring excellence in delivery and value. Below is an overview of our core offerings:

Residential Design & Construction

We design and build custom homes that combine aesthetic excellence with durability, serving clients from Taree to Sydney with a focus on quality and sustainability.

  • Architectural design and 3D modelling for bespoke homes
  • Construction of single-storey, multi-storey, and luxury residences
  • Use of energy-efficient and sustainable materials
  • Full compliance with NSW Building Codes and local regulations
  • Renovations and extensions with minimal disruption

Class 2 & Multi-Residential Construction

We specialise in Class 2 building projects, delivering multi-residential developments across Newcastle, Sydney, and the Mid North Coast to exacting standards.

  • Design and construction of multi-unit residential complexes
  • Management of fire safety, accessibility, and structural compliance
  • Collaboration with architects, engineers, and strata managers
  • High-rise and medium-density development expertise
  • Post-construction maintenance planning

Commercial Construction Management

We manage commercial construction projects, from retail spaces in Coffs Harbour to office builds in Sydney, ensuring timely and cost-effective delivery.

  • Construction of offices, retail spaces, and industrial facilities
  • Fit-out and refurbishment of business premises
  • Coordination of large-scale trades and subcontractors
  • Adherence to workplace health and safety standards
  • Turnkey solutions from planning to occupancy

Estimating & Cost Consulting

Our estimating services offer financial precision for projects in Port Macquarie, Tamworth, and beyond, supporting clients with robust budgeting and cost strategies.

  • Comprehensive cost estimates and feasibility analyses
  • Material take-offs and labour cost projections
  • Risk assessment and contingency budgeting
  • Value management to optimise project economics
  • Real-time cost tracking and reporting

Expert Witness & Dispute Resolution

We provide expert witness services for construction disputes across NSW, offering technical insights and testimony in Sydney, Newcastle, and regional areas.

  • Forensic analysis of defects and delays
  • Expert reports for courts and tribunals
  • Mediation and arbitration support
  • Consultation on contract breaches and compliance
  • Trusted by legal firms, developers, and insurers

Commercial Procurement & Supply Chain Management

We streamline procurement for commercial projects in Forster Tuncurry, Sydney, and regional NSW, securing resources through established networks.

  • Competitive tendering and supplier selection
  • Procurement of specialised materials and equipment
  • Supply chain optimisation for cost and efficiency
  • Contract management and performance monitoring
  • Sustainable sourcing options

Building Surveying & Compliance

Our surveying services ensure compliance and safety for properties in Taree, Port Macquarie, and Sydney, with thorough inspections and recommendations.

  • Building code and Australian Standards audits
  • Dilapidation reports and condition assessments
  • Certification support for occupancy and construction
  • Heritage building assessments

Quantity Surveying & Cost Management

We provide advanced quantity surveying to maximise value, serving clients from Coffs Harbour to Tamworth with financial oversight and planning.

  • Bills of quantities and cost breakdowns
  • Value engineering and cost-benefit analysis
  • Change order management and cost control
  • Cash flow forecasting and risk mitigation
  • Post-completion cost audits

Project Management & Delivery

Our project management ensures seamless delivery of builds across NSW, from planning in Newcastle to handover in Sydney.

  • Stakeholder engagement and communication
  • Quality assurance and defect management
