Who We Are

Caldon Homes Pty Limited is a premier provider of residential construction and commercial building consultancy services, based in Taree, NSW. With a steadfast commitment to quality, precision, and professionalism, we deliver bespoke solutions that exceed client expectations. Our dual expertise in crafting custom homes and providing strategic commercial guidance sets us apart as a trusted partner in the building industry.

As holders of Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873 and Builders Licence 377088C, and proud members of the Housing Industry Association (HIA), we uphold the highest standards of integrity and excellence across all projects.

Our Capabilities and Services

Our comprehensive service offerings span residential construction and commercial consultancy, leveraging decades of industry experience to deliver exceptional results. Below is a detailed overview of our capabilities:

Residential Design & Construction

We specialise in designing and constructing custom homes tailored to individual lifestyles. From initial concept through to final handover, we ensure every detail reflects our clients' vision while adhering to superior standards of craftsmanship and durability.

  • Architectural design and planning tailored to client specifications
  • Construction of single-storey and multi-storey residences
  • Use of premium, sustainable materials for longevity and aesthetics
  • Full compliance with Australian Building Codes and local regulations

Class 2 Building Construction

Our expertise extends to multi-residential projects, including apartments and townhouses. As registered practitioners, we deliver structurally sound, high-quality buildings that meet the complex demands of Class 2 constructions.

  • Construction of multi-unit residential developments
  • Expertise in managing multi-level structural and compliance requirements
  • Integration of modern design with functional living spaces
  • Coordination with architects, engineers, and regulatory bodies

Estimating Services

We provide precise cost estimation and budgeting services for both residential and commercial projects, ensuring financial transparency and control from inception to completion.

  • Detailed cost breakdowns and feasibility studies
  • Accurate forecasting of material, labour, and overhead costs
  • Risk assessment and contingency planning
  • Ongoing cost monitoring and reporting

Expert Witness

Our deep industry knowledge positions us as authoritative expert witnesses in construction-related legal disputes, offering credible, evidence-based testimony to support litigation processes.

  • Preparation of detailed technical reports and assessments
  • Expert testimony in court or mediation proceedings
  • Analysis of construction defects, delays, and disputes
  • Support for builders, developers, and legal teams

Commercial Procurement

We manage procurement and tender processes for commercial projects, securing optimal resources and subcontractor services through strategic negotiation and established industry networks.

  • Tender preparation, evaluation, and award management
  • Sourcing of high-quality materials and specialised trades
  • Cost optimisation without compromising quality
  • Contract negotiation and supplier coordination

Building Surveying

Our building surveying services ensure compliance and safety through meticulous inspections and assessments, identifying issues and verifying adherence to regulatory standards.

  • Comprehensive building condition assessments
  • Defect identification and rectification recommendations
  • Compliance verification with Australian Standards
  • Pre-purchase and due diligence inspections

Quantity Surveying

We offer sophisticated cost management and financial planning, maximising efficiency and profitability through detailed analysis and strategic oversight.

  • Preparation of bills of quantities and cost plans
  • Value engineering to optimise project budgets
  • Cost control and variation management
  • Financial reporting and cash flow projections

Project Management

Our end-to-end project management ensures seamless delivery of residential and commercial builds, coordinating all aspects to meet timelines, budgets, and quality benchmarks.

  • Full project planning and scheduling
  • Stakeholder coordination and communication
  • Quality assurance and risk management
  • Post-construction support and handover
