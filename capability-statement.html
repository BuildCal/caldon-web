Capability Statement

Delivering Expert Commercial Construction and Consultancy Across New South Wales

About CALDON™

CALDON™ is a New South Wales–based firm specializing in commercial building and consultancy services for Class 2, multi-residential, and industrial developments. From Taree to Sydney, we partner with businesses and organizations seeking a high level of expertise in project delivery, compliance, and cost management.

With Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873, Builders Licence 377088C, and membership in the Housing Industry Association (HIA), we uphold the highest standards of compliance, innovation, and professional conduct in every engagement.

Capabilities and Services

CALDON™ provides a comprehensive suite of commercial-focused services. Our approach emphasizes compliance, cost-effectiveness, and collaboration with stakeholders to ensure successful project outcomes.

Class 2 & Multi-Residential

We deliver multi-unit residential developments and Class 2 projects with strict adherence to regulatory requirements and meticulous quality controls.

  • Design and construction of multi-unit complexes
  • Fire safety, accessibility, and structural compliance
  • Collaboration with architects, engineers, and strata
  • Mid-rise and high-rise capabilities
  • Post-completion maintenance planning

Commercial Construction

From offices to industrial developments, we manage every phase of commercial construction, ensuring quality and timely delivery for business-critical projects.

  • Office, retail, and industrial builds
  • Fit-outs and refurbishments
  • Workplace health and safety adherence
  • Subcontractor coordination
  • On-time, on-budget execution

Estimating & Cost Consulting

Our cost consulting services provide financial clarity, from pre-construction feasibility to final account settlement, helping you maintain strong budget control.

  • Feasibility analyses and cost breakdowns
  • Material take-offs and labor estimates
  • Contingency and risk analysis
  • Value engineering for optimal ROI
  • Real-time cost tracking and reporting

Expert Witness & Dispute Resolution

We offer expert opinions and litigation support in construction disputes, ensuring technically sound and impartial assessments for mediation or court proceedings.

  • Forensic investigation of defects and delays
  • Comprehensive expert reports
  • Mediation and arbitration assistance
  • Contract compliance and breach analysis
  • Trusted by legal firms, developers, insurers

Commercial Procurement

Streamline your supply chain and contract management through our procurement services, securing materials and equipment at competitive prices.

  • Competitive tendering and supplier selection
  • Equipment and specialized material sourcing
  • Supply chain optimization
  • Contract performance monitoring
  • Cost-effective, timely procurement

Building Surveying & Compliance

Our surveying services ensure every structure meets relevant codes and regulations, safeguarding the value and longevity of your commercial assets.

  • Pre-purchase, PCI, and defect inspections
  • Building code and Australian Standards audits
  • Dilapidation and condition reports
  • Certification support for occupancy and construction
  • Heritage building assessments

Quantity Surveying & Cost Management

Our quantity surveying solutions ensure accurate budgeting, enabling precise forecasting and strong financial oversight throughout each phase.

  • Bills of quantities and cost breakdowns
  • Change order management and cost tracking
  • Cash flow forecasting
  • Post-completion cost audits
  • Value engineering and cost-benefit analysis

Project Management

Through diligent planning, stakeholder engagement, and risk mitigation, we deliver commercial projects on time and within scope.

  • Scheduling and resource allocation
  • Stakeholder collaboration and reporting
  • Quality assurance and defect control
  • Risk identification and management
  • Post-construction support
