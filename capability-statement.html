About CALDON™

CALDON™ is a leading NSW-based firm specializing in high-quality residential construction and advanced commercial building consultancy. Headquartered in Taree, we extend our expertise across the state, serving key regions including Port Macquarie, Coffs Harbour, Newcastle, Sydney, Tamworth, Forster Tuncurry, and beyond. With a legacy of precision, innovation, and client-centric service, we are your trusted partner for bespoke homes and strategic commercial solutions.

Our credentials—Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873, Builders Licence 377088C, and membership in the Housing Industry Association (HIA)—underscore our commitment to industry-leading standards. CALDON™ combines decades of experience with a forward-thinking approach, ensuring every project reflects excellence in craftsmanship, compliance, and value.

  • Construction of single-storey, multi-storey, and luxury residences
  • Integration of energy-efficient and eco-friendly materials
  • Compliance with NSW Building Codes and local council requirements
