Capability Statement

Delivering Expert Construction and Consultancy Services Across NSW

About CALDON™

CALDON™ is a New South Wales–based firm specialising in consultancy and construction services. Our consultancy offerings extend state‐wide across NSW, providing expert guidance on compliance, cost management, and project coordination for commercial and multi‐residential developments.

For clients seeking construction services—from Port Macquarie to Buladelah, west to Gloucester, and east to Forster Tuncurry—we deliver both residential and commercial builds with meticulous attention to quality and timelines.

With Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873, Builders Licence 377088C, and membership in the Housing Industry Association (HIA), CALDON™ upholds industry‐leading standards in every project.

Capabilities and Services

CALDON™ provides a comprehensive range of solutions, blending our deep technical expertise with a commitment to cost‐effective, compliant delivery. Whether you require state‐wide consultancy or local construction support, we tailor our services to meet your project goals.

We offer select residential builds in our local region, ensuring high‐quality craftsmanship, adherence to building codes, and timely completion.

  • Energy‐efficient materials and design
  • Dedicated project supervision and quality checks

Class 2 & Multi‐Residential

Our multi‐residential capabilities cover everything from design coordination to final handover, ensuring strict compliance with Class 2 regulations.
